Who We Are

RELIABLE HELP
WHEN IT MATTERS

Jireh Roadside Assistance LLC is Dedham's trusted roadside partner — a local, owner-operated service built on speed, honesty, and showing up when it counts. Whether you're stuck on I-95 at midnight or stranded on a quiet side street, we respond fast and treat every call with professionalism.

From a dead battery on a cold winter morning to a blowout on the expressway, our team handles it all: towing, tire changes, lockouts, jump starts, and winch-outs. No AAA membership required — just one call and we come to you, wherever you are.

We serve Dedham and the surrounding Greater Boston area, 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. Real people, real trucks, real help.

What We Do

OUR SERVICES

From quick roadside fixes to full vehicle recovery — we carry the equipment and experience to handle any emergency, day or night.

Towing Service

Safe, professional vehicle towing for cars, trucks, and SUVs. Local and long-distance towing throughout Greater Boston — we get your vehicle where it needs to go, safely.

Tire Change

Blowout or flat on the road? We come to you, swap the spare, and get you back moving fast. No need to wait for a shop — we come to wherever you're stranded.

Lock Out

Locked your keys inside the car? Our technicians use professional tools to open your vehicle quickly and safely — no damage, no drama, back on the road in minutes.

Jump Start

Dead battery killing your plans? We carry commercial-grade jump-start equipment to fire your engine back up — rain, snow, or shine, any time of day or night.

Winch Out

Stuck in mud, snow, or off the road? Our winch-out service recovers vehicles from tough spots safely. We come fully equipped for the extractions that others turn down.
