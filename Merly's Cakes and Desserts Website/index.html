Our Story

Baked with Heart,
Made for Your Moments

At Merly's Cakes and Desserts, every creation starts with one belief: a great cake can make any moment unforgettable. Based in Accra, we specialise in crafting moist, fluffy custom cakes and silky mousse desserts that taste as beautiful as they look.

From intimate birthday gatherings to grand weddings and corporate events, we combine quality ingredients with artisan techniques to deliver cakes that are both stunning and delicious — always at prices that feel as sweet as our treats.
