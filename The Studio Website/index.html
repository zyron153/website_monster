Who We Are

Where creativity comes to life

The Studio is a creative hub of passionate teachers and instructors at the heart of Milnerton, Cape Town. From Dance, Music, Drama and Art to Creative Events and Party Planning — we're where Cape Town comes to create, connect and celebrate imagination.

Driven by Tashai and a team of dedicated artists and educators, The Studio offers internationally accredited courses through Trinity College London, DanceCor, SABOD and more — bringing world-class arts education to your neighbourhood.
